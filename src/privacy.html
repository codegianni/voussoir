Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data Controller

This website, voussoir.eu, is operated by Giovanni Perrini. For any privacy-related matter, you can contact him at g.perrini@pm.me.

2. What Data We Collect and Why

Server and access logs. When you visit this site, Cloudflare (our hosting provider) automatically records your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and the date and time of each request. This data is processed for the legitimate interest of ensuring the security and correct operation of the site. Cloudflare retains access logs for up to 90 days. You can read Cloudflare's privacy policy at cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

Google Fonts. This site loads fonts from Google's CDN (fonts.googleapis.com). When your browser requests a font file, your IP address is transmitted to Google's servers. This is necessary for the site to display correctly and is processed on the basis of legitimate interest. You can read Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Cookie consent preference. When you interact with the cookie banner, your choice (accepted or declined) is saved in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Email correspondence. If you contact us at g.perrini@pm.me, your name, email address, and the content of your message will be processed in order to respond to your enquiry. The legal basis is the performance of pre-contractual steps or, where applicable, our legitimate interest in responding to communications. This data is retained for as long as reasonably necessary.

3. What We Do Not Collect

We do not use analytics cookies, advertising trackers, social media pixels, or any other third-party tracking technology. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for marketing purposes.

4. Your Rights Under the GDPR

If you are located in the European Union or the UK, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — you can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — you can ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Erasure — you can ask us to delete your data, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Restriction — you can ask us to limit how we process your data.
  • Objection — you can object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Portability — you can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at g.perrini@pm.me. We will respond within one month.

5. Supervisory Authority

If you believe your data protection rights have been violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Italian supervisory authority, the Garante per la Protezione dei Dati Personali, at garanteprivacy.it.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised. We encourage you to review it periodically.
