Consulting

Strategic policy expertise for organisations navigating complex governance environments.

Independent consulting to public bodies, foundations, businesses, and third-sector organisations that require substantive policy analysis rather than generic advisory services.

How I work

My approach is grounded in primary source analysis, comparative methodology, and a direct understanding of EU institutional processes. I do not recycle reports. I deliver work that can withstand expert scrutiny and inform real decisions.

Engagements typically begin with a scoping call to clarify the analytical question, the intended audience, and the decision the work needs to support. I work independently or in collaboration with specialist co-authors for broader projects. All deliverables are produced to a standard I would stand behind publicly.
