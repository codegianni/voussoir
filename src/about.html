About

Rigorous analysis. Independent thinking. No agenda.

Voussoir is an independent policy analysis and consulting platform built on one principle: that honest analysis and institutional independence are not in tension — they are the same thing.

GP

Giovanni Perrini

Policy Analyst & Strategic Consultant

I specialise in European governance, digital regulation, and public sector reform. My work draws on a background in political economy, comparative public policy, and European integration, developed at the Università della Svizzera italiana and the University of Bern.

Voussoir was built to produce analysis that is genuinely independent, methodologically rigorous, and written to be read rather than filed. The platform sits at the intersection of academic depth and policy relevance.

Focus Areas

AI & Digital Governance

The EU's emerging regulatory architecture for AI, platforms, and data. From the AI Act to the DGA, tracking how Europe governs the digital economy.

Public Administration & GovTech

Administrative reform, digitalisation of public services, and the governance challenges of technological adoption in the public sector.

EU Policy & Institutions

The mechanics of EU policy processes, institutional dynamics, and how regulatory decisions are made, contested, and implemented.

Geoeconomics & Strategic Autonomy

Trade policy, industrial strategy, and the EU's efforts to build economic and technological sovereignty.
